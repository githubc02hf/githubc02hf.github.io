What is Star Trek

Star Trek is a science fiction universe created by Gene Roddenbery in the 60s as a tv-series and is continued since then with multiple movies and hundreds of hours watchtime and multiple other products to our time. The current series Star Trek: Discovery is being streamed on Netflix.

The Next Generation

The Next Generation is a specific series which takes part in the Star Trek universe. It was created around the 90s. It's captain Jean Luc Picard is cast by Sir Patrick Ian Steward, a nowadays very famous actor, also partially due to his role in this series.

The Series differs to the original series by not including its main star character "Spock", because it plays in a different timeline, than the one of Spock. It's ship the USS Enterprise Galaxy class D is one of the most advanced star ships of that time and its main duty is to explore the edges of the known galaxy to discover new races, ideologies and technologies.
